May 2018

USDA R Workshop
May 29 Get going with R
9:00am - 4:00pm If you have heard of the software R and are interested, but haven't found the time to learn, or you played around with it a bit in the past, but other things have taken priority, or you are (almost) absolutely new to R, this is the course for you! Welcome! In this course, you will learn the basics of R and how to write your own R scripts.
Space
May 30 ggplot2 graphics with R
9:00am - 4:00pm ggplot2: One graphics package to rule them all! The package ggplot2 gives a framework to produce elegant, high-end graphical displays following the technical framework of the Grammar of Graphics (Wilkinson 2000). We will use a lot of data examples to work through the basic plots as well as more advanced tools, such as facetting, colors, shapes and more.
Maps, time series charts and some dynamic graphics? - ggplot2 can help you with that! You will also learn to use the Knitr package to write reports, webpages, and take notes using R. Your analysis will be fully reproducible, so no more wondering what series of commands produced each plot or table - Your documents will be generated dynamically!
Space
May 31 Data Wrasslin' with R
9:00am - 4:00pm Howdy R users! Unorganized and unruly data got you in a pickle? Come on down to the Data Wrasslin' portion of the Week of R and we'll help you learn the tools to quickly and painlessly read, organize, clean, manipulate, and summarize your data. You can put the trials of manipulating data in Excel out of your mind and use your ace in the hole, the tidyverse packages, to get your data into order in minutes. Learn how to read data in directly from Excel files and other common formats. If it takes you forever to get records re-formatted from rows to columns, this is the course for you! Yeehaw!
